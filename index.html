Neil deGrasse Tyson

Author, Astrophysicst, Cosmologist, Earthling

Dr. Neil deGrasse Tyson visited with Goddard's Space Flight Center Director Chris Scolese and the James Webb Space Telescope team at Goddard in Greenbelt, Md. on June 3, 2014.

Life and Career

  • 1958 - Born in New York City
  • 1976 - Graduates from the Bronx High School of Science
  • 1976 - Courted by Carl Sagan to study at Cornell, but ultimately enrolls as a freshman at Harvard University. Studied physics, and was active in dance, rowing and wrestling.
  • 1980 - Graduates from Harvard, begins graduate studies at the University of Texas at Austin
  • 1983 - Graduates from UT Austin with a MA in Astronomy
  • 1986 - Lecturer in Astronomy at the University of Maryland until 1987
  • 1988 - Enrolls at Columbia University, where his work on Type Ia Supernovas leads to the discovery of dark energy.
  • 1991 - PhD in Astrophysics from Columbia University
  • 1992 - Present - Neil brings science to the masses.
